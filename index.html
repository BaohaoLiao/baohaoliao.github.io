Bio

I am currently a software intern in eBay NLP group in Germany. I obtained my Master's degree from the Aachen Institute for Advanced Study in Computational Engineering Science at RWTH Aachen University. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Mechanical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I consider myself as an interdisciplinary researcher. My interdisciplinary background offers me unexhausted ideas when I step into a new area, and prevent me from the curse of knowledge. I am doing a project related to multimodal learning in eBay NLP group. I finished my Master's thesis under the supervision of Prof. Heinz Pitsch, working on the reconstruction of 3D turbulent data. During my master, I also worked as a research assistant with Prof. Hermann Ney, focusing on machine translation and language modeling. My Bachelor's thesis was about cognitive science, more specifically, translating the brain signals to output commands for controlling mechanical equipments.
